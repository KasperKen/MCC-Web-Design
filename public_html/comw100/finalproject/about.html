About Us

We are a family owned and operated detail cleaning company that opened in 2014. We started out cleaning apartments and washing windows before rapid growth prompted us to open a second business focusing on Highrise work. We specialize in the cleaning of government and commercial buildings, and 90% of our work comes from returning customers.
